## System Purpose

**System type** – Single-page web application (SPA) built with React, organized in modular components, hooks, services and context-based state management.

The platform is a Customer Success (CS) application that consolidates the core workflows needed to manage client relationships and internal CS operations.

Its architecture reveals a set of cohesive modules that together deliver:

- Client Management
- Project & Activity Tracking
- Health Scoring & Monitoring
- Onboarding & Activation
- Operational Dashboard
- Reporting & Export
- Internal Assistant – Donkie

**Problem solved** – The system eliminates fragmented spreadsheets and disparate tools by providing a unified workspace where CS professionals can manage clients, monitor health metrics, track activities and projects, and generate reports.

**Primary users** – Customer Success managers, CS operations analysts, and support agents.

**Operational goal** – Enable CS teams to maintain visibility over customer health, reduce churn risk, and streamline operational workflows through a centralized platform.
